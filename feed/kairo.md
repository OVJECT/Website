# KAIRO™ - Cinematic Film Camera

**The warmth of analog, captured on your phone.**

KAIRO™ is a photography application designed to evoke the nostalgic feel of old film cameras. It captures moments with natural colors, soft light leaks, and subtle grain, leaving a deep and lasting impression.

### Key Features
*   **Film Aesthetic**: Carefully calibrated to mimic the unique characteristics of analog film.
*   **Natural Light Leaks**: Adds organic warmth and texture to your photos.
*   **Subtle Grain**: For that authentic vintage look.
*   **Simple Operation**: Point, shoot, and develop memories instantly.
