# PITAPATO : Mole Out the Barrel

**Pop! Goes the mole.**

A cheeky digital reimagining of the classic barrel-popping toy. PITAPATO brings surprise and suspense to your fingertips. Tap to poke, cross your fingers, and pray the mole doesn’t fly out!

### Key Features
*   **Pirate Roulette**: Insert the knife, survive the round.
*   **Cute Characters**: Featuring the charming Pitapato mole.
*   **Party Ready**: Quick rounds, high tension, instant fun.
*   **Custom Rules**: Make it more interesting with your own penalty rules.
