# Random TapTap

**Decisions made simple. And fun.**

Random TapTap is a straightforward yet effective random picker game for group decisions, party entertainment, and social fun. Just touch the screen and let the particles decide.

### Key Features
*   **Intuitive Gameplay**: Just place your fingers on the screen.
*   **Fair & Random**: Algorithm ensures equal chances for everyone.
*   **Visual Delight**: Smooth animations and sparkling particle effects.
*   **Multiplayer**: Supports up to 10 simultaneous players.
*   **No Ads**: Free to play with zero interruptions.
